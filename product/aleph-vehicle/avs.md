A set of smart contracts that manage vault allocations, participantsâ€™ accounting and serves as an entry point for Stakers.

Aleph develops a custom AVS with an embedded synthetic Operator that serves as an entry point for vault deposits. The AVS enables Restakers to allocate LST to Aleph Vaults, and functions as a trustless issuer of tokenized proof of redistributed collateral. The system manages accounting records for all participants, calculates Staker rewards

A yield-bearing representation of staked LST in EigenLayer that serves as tokenized proof of redistributed collateral. The alETH represents the ownership of an individual Restaker in AlephVault.
